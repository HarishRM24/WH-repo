Media round up on the World Happiness Report 2021

Share on Twitter, LinkedIn

In observance of the U.N. International Day of Happiness on March 20, the Sustainable Development Solutions Network (SDSN), Center for Sustainable Development at Columbia University (CSD), and partners of the World Happiness Report (WHR) hosted a launch of the 2021 World Happiness Report. As we are living through an exceptional time dealing with the widespread effects of the pandemic, this year’s World Happiness Report discussed world happiness during COVID-19.

For media inquiries on the WHR 2021 please reach out to info@worldhappiness.report or Kyu Lee at media@worldhappiness.report.
